About R'pet Station

Welcome to our innovative web-based appointment system designed for R'Pet Station. Our platform seamlessly integrates QR code and SMS technology to provide a convenient and efficient way for pet owners to book appointments for their furry friends.


With just a few clicks, users can schedule their appointments online, receive real-time reminders via SMS, and easily access their booking details through a personalized QR code. Whether you're scheduling a routine checkup, grooming, or other pet services, our system simplifies the process, saving you time and ensuring a smooth experience.
